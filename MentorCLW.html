About the Curator

Dr. Sarah Jenkins has spent over 15 years bridging the gap between high-potential students and elite academic funding. As a curator on Scholar Stream, she focuses on high-impact STEM opportunities that offer more than just financial support, but also a network for lifelong research and professional growth.

Her sessions focus on the 'hidden curriculum' of scholarship applications: crafting a personal brand that resonates with selection committees while maintaining authentic academic integrity.
